Lemon Battery Project

In this project we were supposed to make a battery out of any item that can produce a charge. For this project I used a lemon! At first I used a cucumber just to expirement but doing so I found that they also produce a charge. With further research I found that lemons produce more currents than any other fruits. So we went to the store and bought about ten lemons. With those lemons they produced about six and a half volts of electricty. However, it didn't light the LED that we were supposed to make light up.
